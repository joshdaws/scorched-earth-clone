## Technical Stack Options:

### Option 1: Web-to-Mobile (Fastest)
- **Framework**: Current codebase + Capacitor
- **Pros**: Reuse 90% of current code, fast to market
- **Cons**: Performance limitations, harder to monetize
- **Timeline**: 2-3 months

### Option 2: Unity (Industry Standard)
- **Framework**: Unity + C#
- **Pros**: Best performance, asset store, easy monetization
- **Cons**: Complete rewrite needed
- **Timeline**: 4-6 months

### Option 3: Godot (Open Source)
- **Framework**: Godot + GDScript
- **Pros**: Free, good 2D support, growing community
- **Cons**: Smaller ecosystem, learning curve
- **Timeline**: 3-5 months
